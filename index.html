Before You Start

Before you even start to create content, you need to think of what content you want to create. After you figure that out, you need to make sure that whatever content you're making, you enjoy making it. If you don't appreciate your content, no one will.

Recording Devices

The first thing you'll need after having content is a way to capture it. Which means you'll need a recording device. Your device will change depending on what type of content you're creating. Whether you're a Gamer or a Vlogger, I have some great suggestions for you.

Smartphones
A Smartphone is a device found in almost every person's front or back pocket. And if you're thinking about making some content for social media, chances are you have one. This simple fact is why this is the easiest way to share content.
Readmore
Digital Cameras
A Digital Camera is a big step up from a mobile phone. I would only suggest investing in one if
1. You have the money to spend.
2. You have experience using professional cameras
3. You have experience editing photos/videos.
Readmore
Capture Cards*
A Capture Card is an absolute necessity if you play on either Nintendo or Xbox. Since these companies have little to no share features, you'll have to record your content with a Capture Card. Elgato is currently the leading brand in the industry.
Readmore

*You don't need a Capture Card if you're on PlayStation.
Just hit the Share Button and Record!
