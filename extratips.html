Here are a few extra applications & tips that may increase productivity or just help you relax.

Spotify
Spotify is a great music app to use while editing. But if you can't or won't download Spotify, music in general can help you relax or get into the 'zone' which can only help you while editing content.

Scriptwriting Software*
Scriptwriting software can help content creators that struggle with instinctively speaking. It allows you to focus on other parts of your content without having to worry about what you're saying.

Listen to a Podcast

Listening to Podcasts can help you feel relaxed. The best thing about podcasts is that they cater to your interests. All you have to do is find a topic you enjoy, and 9 times out of 10 there's a podcast for it. And it's like having friends to talk to while you edit.
